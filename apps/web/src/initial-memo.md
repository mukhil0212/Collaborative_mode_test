Welcome! We're excited to have you join our organization. This guide will help you get started and make your first days as smooth as possible.

## First Week Checklist

1. Complete HR paperwork and setup
2. Review company policies and handbook
3. Set up your workspace and equipment
4. Meet your team members
5. Schedule 1-on-1s with key stakeholders
